Activity Ideas for Mole Day!
  • Hole Digging Competition- Host a hole digging competition in which participants must race to dig a 1/2 meter deep hole. The first to sucessfully dig the hole wins!

  • Face Painting- Participants must form groups of 2 people, and one of the group members must paint the other's face to look like a mole. Judges will decide which group has the best mole face

  • Mole Jokes- In groups of 2 or 3 people, participants must come up with a mole-related joke. This can be a pun, reference, or any other kind of joke that is relevant to Mole Day, Avogadro, or the Mol unit. Judges must pick who has the best joke and wins the competition.

  • Make Guacamole- Guacamole is a great dish to make, especially on Mole Day. If you're not a fan of competitions, this can easily be hosted as a simple cooking of guacamole, without each dish being judged.

  • Mole Day Rap- participants have to come up with a Mole Day themed song, in groups of 3 or 4 people. The song itself does not have to be a rap, though bonus points should be given for rhyming.
